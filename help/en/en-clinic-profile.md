# Editing Clinic Information

Here you manage your clinicâ€™s identity details that appear in the system, in offer forms, and in automated messages sent to patients.

**Path:** `My Clinic > Clinic Profile`

## Update Information

1. Click **Edit Company** in the top-right corner.
2. In the form that opens, make your changes:
   * **Clinic Name:** The name shown in the system.
   * **Address/Location:** The address required for maps and communication.
   * **Email:** The email address that will appear in notifications sent to patients.
   * **Phone:** The contact phone number.
3. Click **Save** to finish.
