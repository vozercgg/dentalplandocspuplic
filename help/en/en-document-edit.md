
# Editing the Document (Editor)

After selecting pages, you can personalize the document on the editor screen.

**Path:** `Cases > [Patient Name] > Document Pages > (Editor)`

## Editing Images and Text

* **Edit Text:** Double-click the text you want to edit. You can update price explanations, headings, or patient-specific notes.
* **Add/Replace Images:** From **Uploaded Photos** in the left panel, you can upload your own visuals, or use the **Stock Photos** gallery.

## Color Settings

From the top menu, use **Template Color** to match the document’s main color with your clinic brand identity.

Don’t forget to click **Save** on the top bar to keep your changes.
