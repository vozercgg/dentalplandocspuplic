
# Sharing and Downloading the Document

There are three ways to deliver the offer you prepared to the patient.

**Path:** `Cases > [Patient Name] > Document Pages > (Editor)`

## 1) Download as PDF

To download the document to your computer, click **PDF** on the top bar. The high-resolution file downloads within seconds.

## 2) Digital Sharing (WhatsApp, Link, QR)

When you click **Share Doc**, the sharing window opens:

* **Whatsapp:** Sends an automatic message and link to the patientâ€™s number.
* **QR Code:** Scan the QR code to view
* **Copy Link:** Copy the link and send it manually via SMS or email.
