# Adding and Tracking Payments

Use this to record collections received from the patient (Cash, Credit Card, etc.) and keep the balance up to date.

**Path:** `Cases > [Patient Name] > Treatment`

## Record a Payment

1. From the action panel on the right, click **Add Payment**.
2. In the window that opens, fill in the required fields:
   * **Payment Date:** The date the payment was received.
   * **Payment Type:** Choose Cash, Credit Card, Bank Transfer, etc.
   * **Payment Amount:** Enter the amount received.
   * **Currency:** Make sure it matches the planâ€™s currency.
   * **Description:** (Optional) Add a note (e.g., *Deposit*, *Installment 1*).
3. Click **Save**.

## Financial Status (Balance Tracking)

After adding a payment, the summary table at the bottom updates:

* **Total Due:** Total plan amount (including discounts).
* **Total Paid:** Total of payments entered so far.
* **Remaining Balance:** Amount the patient still needs to pay.
