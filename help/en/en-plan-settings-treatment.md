# Treatment and Price List Settings

Here you define the standard prices, names, and patient-facing descriptions of the treatments used in your clinic.

**Path:** `Plan Settings > Treatment`

## General Settings (Language and Currency)

Before you start editing the list, check the top bar:

* **Treatment Language:** Select the language you are preparing the list in (e.g., TR or EN). Patient documents are based on this language.
* **Currency:** Select the default currency for prices (USD, EUR, TRY, etc.).
* **Save:** To apply your changes, click **Save** before leaving the page.

## Add a New Treatment

1. Find the relevant treatment group (e.g., *Filling*, *Implant*).
2. Click **Add Treatment** on the right side of the group.
3. Fill in the new row:
   * **Treatment Name:** The technical name you will see.
   * **Patient Description:** A simple explanation the patient will see in PDFs and Animation (e.g., *A filling is applied to your tooth*).
   * **Price:** The standard list price.

## Edit and Duplicate

To edit an existing treatment, click the field and type.

* **Change Model:** Select which 3D tooth model will be used in the animation.
* **Clone:** Duplicates the row to create a similar treatment quickly.
* **Delete:** Removes the treatment from the list.
