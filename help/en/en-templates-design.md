
# Page Design and Editing

Each page that forms the PDF document (Cover, Intro, Treatment list, etc.) is listed as a separate card.

**Path:** `Side Menu > Templates`

## Editing Pages

1. Under the page card you want to edit, click **Edit**.
2. In the editor, you can:
   * **Images:** Add your clinic photos or logo.
   * **Text:** Update headings and fixed descriptions.
   * **Colors:** Use color blocks that match your brand.
