# Creating and Sharing a Treatment Animation

Turn the treatment plan you created into a 3D animation that your patient can easily understand. This feature is especially useful for inviting the patient to the clinic or visualizing the treatment process.

**Path:** `Cases > [Patient Name] > Animation`

## Generate the Animation

1. First, prepare your plan in the **Treatment** tab.
2. From the top menu, click the **Animation** tab.
3. The system automatically generates a 3D animation based on your plan.

## Add AI Narration

When sending the animation to the patient, you can also add a professional text that explains the treatment.

1. On the animation screen, click **Generate AI Treatment Story**.
2. The AI generates a story and an explanation text suitable for the treatment plan.
3. You can edit and personalize the text as you like.

## Share the Animation with the Patient

To send the animation, click **Share & Save Animation** in the top-right corner.

Choose the option that suits you:

### 1) Send via WhatsApp
The fastest method. When you press **Whatsapp**, a message draft and the animation link are prepared automatically for the patientâ€™s number. You only need to press send.

### 2) Show with QR Code
If the patient is in the clinic, have them scan the **QR Code** shown on the screen with their phone camera and watch the animation on their own device.

### 3) Copy the Link
Click **Copy Link** to copy the URL, then send it manually via Email, SMS, or Instagram DM.
