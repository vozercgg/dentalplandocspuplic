# Applying a Discount to the Treatment Plan

On this screen you can apply two discount types: to the overall total or only to selected rows.

**Path:** `Cases > [Patient Name] > Treatment` (Subtotal area)

---

## 1) Total Discount (On the Overall Amount)

To apply a discount on the final total of the plan:

1. Click **Total Discount** at the bottom.
2. Choose a type: **Percentage (%)** or **Fixed Amount (â‚º/â‚¬/$)**.
3. Enter the discount value.
4. Confirm.

---

## 2) Edit Prices (Row-based Discount)

To apply a discount only to specific procedures (e.g., implants only):

1. Click **Edit Prices** at the bottom.
2. Tick the **checkbox** at the beginning of the rows you want to discount.
3. Enter the rate or amount in the discount field at the bottom of the panel.
4. Click **Apply Discount**.
