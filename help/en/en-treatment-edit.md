# Editing and Deleting the Plan

You can change the order of rows added to the plan or delete items that were added by mistake.

## Change Order

1. Find the row you want to move.
2. **Press and hold** the row.
3. Drag it up or down and drop it where you want.

## Delete a Treatment

1. Hover over the row you want to delete.
2. Click the **Delete (Trash)** icon on the right side of the row.
3. Confirm to remove the item.

> ℹ️ **Info:** These actions only change the current patient’s plan.
